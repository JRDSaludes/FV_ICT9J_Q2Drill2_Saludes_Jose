About Us


OB Montessori Fairview offers a caring, child-centered Montessori education that encourages independence, curiosity, and a love of learning. Our Montessori-trained teachers provide hands-on, individualized lessons in a prepared environment that supports academic, social, and emotional growth. We partner closely with families to create a safe, welcoming community where each child can thrive.
