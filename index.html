OB Montessori Fairview is a child-centered Montessori school offering hands-on, individualized learning for preschool and early elementary students. We emphasize independence, critical thinking, and social-emotional growth through prepared classrooms led by experienced Montessori-trained teachers. Our welcoming campus and strong parent partnerships support each child's curiosity, creativity, and lifelong love of learning.
